About Me

It's common for people to start talking about what they do for a living when describing themselves to others, which obviously makes sense in certain situations (i.e. work-related situations) but, it's something that I'm trying to stop doing. I don't want to be identified by what I do at work, even if my work is important to me. There are a lot of things about me that I like to share and find people with similarities to interact with.
